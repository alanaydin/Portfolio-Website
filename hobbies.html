Hobbies

My fav things to do

Football has always been a huge part of my life. Ever since I was a kid, I dreamed of becoming a footballer like my idol, Lionel Messi. But for a long time, I never had the confidence or the opportunity to join a club. At 15, I finally took the step and played for a club for over a year. Eventually, I stopped due to a lack of time and the realization that going pro wasn’t realistic for me.

Besides football, I’m really into movies and video games. I’m more of a competitive gamer, but I also enjoy a good story-driven game every now and then.

Superheroes are another big passion of mine—I love reading comics, watching superhero movies, and keeping up with the latest shows.

Music is a huge part of my life as well, especially hip-hop, rap, and R&B. Some of my favorite artists include Travis Scott, A$AP Rocky, and Frank Ocean. Their music inspires and motivates me daily.

Right now, I work 4 to 5 days a week, so my free time is limited. But when I do have time, I like to hit the gym or just chill with my friends. No matter how busy life gets, I always find a way to enjoy the things I love.
